## HIGH PRIORITY (DR 50+, good traffic, dofollow)

### 1. Peerlist
- **URL:** https://peerlist.io/
- **Free/Paid:** Free
- **DR:** ~75
- **Backlinks:** Nofollow
- **How to submit:** Sign up, complete profile, submit product via "Projects" section
- **Approval time:** 1-3 days
- **Notes:** Professional network for builders. Large active community. Good for founder visibility.

### 2. SaaSBison
- **URL:** https://saasbison.com/
- **Free/Paid:** Free tier available (paid for premium features)
- **DR:** Unknown (newer directory)
- **Backlinks:** Dofollow (3 permanent backlinks per listing)
- **How to submit:** Submit form on website
- **Approval time:** 1-5 days
- **Notes:** Curated directory with AI-powered reviews. Claims visibility on Google, ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.

### 3. IdeaKiln
- **URL:** https://ideakiln.com/
- **Free/Paid:** Free (1 idea/week on free tier)
- **DR:** ~55
- **Backlinks:** Dofollow
- **How to submit:** Sign up, submit product. No verification badge required.
- **Approval time:** 1-3 days
- **Notes:** Community-driven directory for early-stage products. Includes timeline updates, discount codes, and community feedback. Good for building in public.

### 4. StartupTrusted
- **URL:** https://startuptrusted.com/
- **Free/Paid:** Free
- **DR:** ~53
- **Backlinks:** Dofollow
- **How to submit:** Submit form on website
- **Approval time:** 2-5 days
- **Notes:** Verified startup database with categorized listings. Emphasizes trusted discovery. Founded 2025.

### 5. TheSaaSDirectory
- **URL:** https://thesaasdirectory.com/

### 6. SaaSDB

## NOTES

- Spread submissions over 2-3 weeks to avoid appearing spammy to search engines
- For each submission, use consistent branding: name, description, logo, screenshots
- Track which directories provide dofollow vs nofollow links after listing goes live
- Monitor referral traffic from each directory in analytics
- Several directories listed here were discovered via curated lists on LaunchDirectories.com, Antforms, and StartupSauce
