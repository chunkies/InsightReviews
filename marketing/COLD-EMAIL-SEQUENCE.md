## Sending Notes

- **Spacing:** Email 1 on Day 0, Email 2 on Day 3, Email 3 on Day 8.
- **Threading:** Email 2 and 3 should reply to the original thread (same subject with "Re:") to boost open rates.
- **Personalisation:** Always reference their actual Google rating. Generic emails get binned.
- **Time to send:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7:00-8:30am local time (before the morning rush).
- **Volume:** Keep under 30/day per sending address to protect deliverability.
- **Remove:** Anyone who replies, clicks, or unsubscribes. Check Google rating is still accurate before sending.
