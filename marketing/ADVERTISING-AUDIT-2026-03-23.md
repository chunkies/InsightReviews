## Ad Creative Assessment

### What's Working
- Opening hook: "Your Google rating is costing you customers"
- 88% statistic adds credibility
- Naming business types (cafes, salons, dentists, gyms)
- Price transparency ($79/mo) — filters out non-buyers

### What Needs Improvement

- Audience Network wasting budget

## Growing Followers & Reviews (Strategies)

### Organic (Free)
- Invite all personal FB friends to like the page
- Follow 100-200 Melbourne businesses on Instagram daily
- Comment meaningfully on local business posts
- Share page link in Melbourne business Facebook Groups
- Ask friends/family/beta users for FB recommendations
- Cross-post between Facebook and Instagram

### Paid (Low Budget)
- $20-30 Page Likes campaign targeting Melbourne small business owners (1-2 weeks)
- Boost best-performing post ($5-10)
- Do NOT buy fake followers/reviews — Meta detects and penalizes this

### Reviews Specifically
- Ask anyone who's seen the product demo
- Ask friends who own businesses
- Ask in Melbourne entrepreneur communities
- Offer to leave a review on their business in exchange (reciprocal, not fake)
