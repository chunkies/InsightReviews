# Competitor Landscape

## Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Target | Monthly Price | Key Differentiator |
|-----------|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Birdeye** | Mid-market, multi-location | $300-600+/mo | All-in-one: reviews, listings, social, webchat |
| **Podium** | Local businesses (dentists, auto) | $300-600+/mo | SMS-first: reviews, payments, webchat |
| **NiceJob** | Small service businesses | $75-125/mo | Review collection + automated referrals |
| **ReviewGain** | Local businesses + agencies | From $49/mo | Whitelabel agency option |
| **Riivu** | Local businesses | Not listed | Email/text/QR review collection |
| **Reviewly AI** | Local businesses | Not listed | AI-drafted review suggestions |
| **InsightReviews (us)** | Local businesses (AU-first) | $79/mo | SMS smart-routing, negative review interception, simple |

## Our Positioning

- **Price advantage** vs Birdeye/Podium ($79 vs $300+)
- **Simplicity advantage** vs all — staff can use in 5 seconds, no training
- **Australia-first** — local support, AU phone numbers, AU-centric
- **Smart routing** as core differentiator — not just collection, but interception of negative reviews

## What NOT to say

- Never badmouth competitors by name
- Never call it "review gating" — use "smart routing" or "review collection"
- Position as "purpose-built for single-location businesses" vs "enterprise tools for small business budgets"
