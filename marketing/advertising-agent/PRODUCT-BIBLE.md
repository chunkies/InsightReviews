# InsightReviews — Product Bible

## What It Is

InsightReviews is a review collection and smart routing platform for local businesses. A clerk enters a customer's phone number at the till, the system sends an SMS with a branded review link, and smart-routes the response: positive reviews (4-5 stars) get redirected to Google, Yelp, Facebook, TripAdvisor etc. Negative reviews (1-3 stars) stay private so the business can follow up.

## Who It's For

Local brick-and-mortar businesses: cafes, salons, dentists, gyms, auto shops. Australia-first, but works globally. Target: single-location businesses that want more 5-star reviews and want to catch bad experiences before they go public.

## Key Features

- **SMS review requests** — Staff enters phone number, customer gets a text in seconds
- **Smart routing** — Happy customers → Google/Yelp. Unhappy customers → private feedback
- **Negative review interception** — Catch bad experiences before they become 1-star public reviews
- **Multi-platform** — Route to Google, Yelp, Facebook, TripAdvisor, or any custom URL
- **Testimonial wall** — Curate best reviews into an embeddable "wall of love"
- **Dead simple UX** — Staff can use it in 5 seconds, no training needed
- **Dashboard** — Review stats, trends, response tracking

## Tech Stack

Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Supabase (PostgreSQL), Stripe, Twilio, MUI v7, deployed on Vercel.
