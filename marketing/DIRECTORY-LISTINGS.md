## Key Tips

- **Capterra is the single highest-ROI action** — covers 4 platforms in one submission
- Collect reviews on Capterra/G2 immediately after listing — empty profiles rank poorly
- Use consistent branding across all listings (same logo, tagline, screenshots)
- Track referral traffic with UTM parameters
- Respond to every review on every platform

## Product Hunt Launch Checklist

- [ ] Build 50+ followers on upcoming page first
- [ ] Prepare: tagline (60 chars), description, 5+ screenshots/GIF
- [ ] Write maker comment explaining the "why"
- [ ] Schedule for Tuesday-Thursday at 12:01 AM PT
- [ ] Extended trial offer for PH users
- [ ] Have launch-day support ready to respond to every comment
- [ ] Engage on PH for 2-4 weeks before launch
