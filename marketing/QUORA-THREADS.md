## Key Stats to Use in Answers

- 98% of texts get opened (vs ~20% for email)
- 74% of consumers only trust reviews from the last 3 months
- 47% won't use a business with fewer than 20 reviews
- 0.5-star increase = 5-9% revenue increase (Michael Luca, Harvard)
- 4.2-4.5 star range is the most trusted (perfect 5.0 reduces trust)
- Only 38% of small businesses have a structured review management strategy
